About

Assessment and monitoring of drought related indices are essential for the sustainable management of agricultural resources. This is essential in the Hindu Kush-Himalayan region as a lot of people in the region are dependent on agriculture as primary source of their income.

The application provides easy access to various indices related to drought such as precepitation, temperature, soil moisture, evapotranspiration and spi. It provides user friendly tools for generating statistics to understand the change processes and to support the informed decisions
